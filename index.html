About Us

About RSN Enterprise

With extensive experience in sourcing and supply chain management, we specialize in the import, export, and local trading of a wide range of agro commodities, including rice, beans, lentils, spices, dry fruits, and vegetable oils. Our strong supplier network and customer-focused approach enable us to provide high-quality products at competitive prices.

In addition to agricultural commodities, RSN Enterprise is actively engaged in the trading of herbal and personal care products such as mehndi powder, mehndi cones, fast color cones, herbal hair colors, and herbal creams. We are dedicated to maintaining high standards of quality, consistency, and customer satisfaction in every product we offer.

Since our establishment, we have built a reputation for integrity, professionalism, and dependable business practices, serving clients across various industries and markets.

  • Rice, beans, lentils, spices & dry fruits
  • Vegetable oils & agro commodities
  • Mehndi powder, cones & fast color cones
  • Herbal hair colors & herbal creams

Authorized Channel Partner

RSN Enterprises & Neha Herbals

RSN Enterprises is the Authorized Channel Partner and distributor of Neha Herbals Pvt. Ltd., India, for the international marketing and export of the complete range of Neha Herbal products.

With the authorization of Neha Herbals, RSN Enterprises is responsible for promoting, supplying, and expanding the global presence of Neha products across international markets. We specialize in serving customers in Libya and the Middle East, while also catering to other overseas markets.

Backed by the quality and reputation of Neha Herbals, we are committed to delivering genuine products, reliable supply, and excellent customer service to our international partners and distributors.

  • Libya — primary export market
  • Middle East (UAE, Iraq, Saudi Arabia, Oman, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, Jordan, etc.)
  • Africa & other international markets
  • Hair Color Cream & Hair Color Powder
  • Henna Powder & Herbal Hair Care Products
  • Complete Neha Herbals Product Range
